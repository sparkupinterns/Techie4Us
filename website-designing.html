Website Designing FAQ's

Do you provide SEO-friendly websites?

Yes, we do provide SEO services and SEO-friendly websites to bring traffic to your website and have excellent customer engagement. Along with full digital marketing services as per your need.

How much will it take to redesign my existing website?

Again, it totally depends on what kind of improvements you require. But, you might contact us for the same.

How much does a website design cost?

The cost of website design will directly depend upon the type and need. But, it approximately ranges from INR 7,000 to 5,00,000. Our static website design initiates from INR 7,500 and dynamic from INR 35,000. The total costs mainly depend on the sections that are there on the website. You can also avail of the website design packages provided by us.

How long does it take to build a new website?

Website designing take time, but it depends upon the type of website design you opt for. A week or less might take for a basic two-page website with a predetermined design and features. Usually, 10-12 weeks are required to build an advanced website with a modification time of 2 weeks.

Do you provide website design samples?

Yes, you can check for our website design samples at our website design portfolio page.

What will be the maintenance cost of my website?

The maintenance cost of your website will depend upon its existing features on it. In most of the cases where SAM Web Studio has developed a website, 25% to 30% of the total website development cost will be charged as an AMC account.
