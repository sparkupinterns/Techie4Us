This is one of the golden opportunities for all those photographers who have excellent photography skills. Nowadays the trend of professional photographers are going on in the modern world but when we take a look on the real platforms that are giving the rewards and awards to the photographers we can say that there are no options available for the users. This website is offering you the chance to participate in the photography competition and win the awards. If you have skills of excellent photography then you must participate in the competition to achieve awards.

Marketing Strategy

  • You should submit your photographs or creations which you click on your professional camera.
  • Submit your collection on the Indian Photography for participating in the competition in a hassle free manner as it has been designed in laravel framework.
  • If you are selected as the winner in the Indian Photography Awards, you will be awarded by the best certifications.

Technology Used

  1. HTML5
  2. CSS3
  3. JAVASCRIPT
  4. BOOTSTRAP
  5. Laravel Framework is used in the website for backend process.
  6. The most demandable database is used in the website which is MySql.
  7. The payments are safe and secure on API Payment Gateway.

Objective

  • The main goal of the platform is to offer the achievements in the form of awards to the professional photographers.
  • The skills of photographs are appreciated and they are rewarded accordingly
  • To reward the talent and hard work is the first and foremost aim.
