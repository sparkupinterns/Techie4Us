What is Search Engine Optimization?

Definition- Search Engine Optimization in short SEO. We call it’s an art of ranking higher on a search engine in an organic way.
Let’s break down further.
Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a Digital Marketing technique of optimizing your website content in relation to relevant and frequently searched keywords or phrases so that the search engine (Google, Bing, and Yahoo, etc.) like to show your content on the top of searches with free and unlimited traffic that brings valuable customers and sales.
In Short, when someone enters the keyword, phase in the search engine, then your website will appear on the first place of the results.
A Higher SERP (Search Engine Result Page) puts your website in front of those people who are looking for the products & services that are offered by you.

How Does SEO Work?

SEO which stands for Search engine optimization is the process of optimizing a website to maximize the organic traffic from search engines. SEO is what helps a website in achieving better search engine rankings and achieve more traffic with the ultimate goal of generating more business and engagement. Think of a website that is content-rich and highly informative and yet it does not appear anywhere near the top when a topic similar to it is searched on Google, Yahoo, Firefox, etc. What and how will this website appear on top, and what will help it reach more engagement and traffic? The answer is SEO. The website may have been content-rich, what it was missing was optimization of the specific keywords and a better user experience which SEO can seamlessly correct.

What are the types of SEO services?

The 3 Types of SEO services that help in optimizing a website for better engagement and a well-rounded organic search strategy are:

  • On-Page SEO

    On-Page SEO targets to optimize an individual page of the website. It requires the step by step process of researching the keywords that can be potentially targeted on the webpage and then working around those keywords to create quality content. And then optimizing the keywords using the meta tag SEO.

  • Technical SEO

    Technical SEO, as the name suggests refers to the non-content or technical parts of the website. It is used to improve the site’s backend foundation and structure. It improves the site’s readability and enhances the user experience. A good user experience can affect overall traffic and engagement which helps search engines see that the site is of high quality.

  • Off-Page SEO

    Off-Page SEO is how your brand can accumulate customer’s trust and earn its credibility. It uses high–quality backlinks to show the users that the website is an ideal search result for it is from a reputable, and trusted source.

What’s the Difference between On-page SEO and Off-Page SEO?

On-Page SEO, Those activities that directly affect your website (Content and HTML source code) that can be optimized. Some more Key things that you need to optimize such as Website sped, titles, description, keyword use in your content, images, internal linking, etc. As Google becomes more sophisticated that the user to be satisfied when they visit your website. Off-Page SEO- Those activities that are done outside the website can be optimized. These are social bookmarking, forum marketing, blog posting, article submission, social media marketing, link baiting, search engine submission, and many more.
