Social media marketing is the process of using social media platforms to promote your business and generate leads. It involves making strategies and leading effective social media campaigns to enhance your brand reach and impact.

It involves more active and engaging participation with your social media account. You nurture your social media profile by creating posts, scheduling them, engaging customers, and monitoring progress.
