WooCommerce vs Shopify: Which eCommerce Platform is Best For Your Business?

04 Jul, 2024

When searching for the top eCommerce platform, Shopify consistently ranks top among all other website builders available. Shopify is undoubtedly one of the greatest platforms for online businesses. It offers many advantages, including scalable design and responsive layouts. 

Despite having many advanced features, Shopify also has many drawbacks, which makes it not profitable in many cases. This is why you should take your steps carefully before building and managing your platform with it. The perfect method to choose the best eCommerce platform is to compare Shopify with the other popular solutions. 

Every business has its unique requirements. If one ecommerce platform suites someone, it doesn’t mean the same will be useful for your business type. So, if you are turning to Shopify like other people, wait a minute. What is about its comparison with WooCommerce? 

In contrast to Shopify, which is a hosted platform made exclusively for online sales, WooCommerce is a WordPress-based ecommerce plugin. It is the most popular ecommerce plugging that is used by more than 6 million users in the world. WooCommerce is an easy-to-use platform where you just need to choose a website and host, install plugins, and you may need to code some lines to customize your shop. 

If you want to explore WooCommerce and Shopify to choose the best one, check out this guide to learn who provides the best functionality and website security measures. Here, we’ll get to know which platform is better for your online store. So let’s get started!

Shopify vs WooCommerce: Which Is Best For You?

WooCommerce remains the most popular ecommerce platform globally with a 38.74% market share. Whereas Shopify is the fourth leading ecommerce platform worldwide with a 10.32% market share. Shopify features like the new Checkout Sheet Kit that enhances mobile app checkouts and boosts conversions by 15%.

Here is a compressive comparison of both WooCommerce and Shopify across various features to understand their difference in a better manner:

1.Dropshipping

Dropshipping is a popular business model in which an online store sells products without keeping them in stock. The store owner never handles the product directly. Instead, when a customer purchases an item, a third party (supplier) ships it directly to the customer.


WooCommerce

It is a well-known platform for dropshipping. You can install and integrate numerous dropshipping extensions from the WordPress repository. It allows you to add third-party extensions to your online platform, which makes it a flexible option for dropshipping. Here are some top third-party WooCommerce extensions include:

  • AliDropship
  • DropshipMe
  • YouDroop
  • WooDropship 

Shopify

It is a cutting-edge solution for dropshipping. It provides many extensions and add-ons to streamline the dropshipping process. You can add these best Shopify dropshipping add-ons:

  • CJDropshipping Spocket
  • MIIUT: Unlimited Dropshipping 
  • Ali Orders AliExpress Dropship

Shopify also supports third-party dropshipping marketplaces such as AliExpress, Oberlo, and Printify.

Both WooCommerce and Shopify are excellent options for running a dropshipping business. However, in many cases, you can find WooCommerce more beneficial than Shopify because of its ease of use and vast number of extensions. If your business is dropshipping-focused, WooCommerce can provide you with more customizable and flexible options. However, Shopify has limited options and may not be perfect for dropshipping.

2.Cost

When starting an ecommerce online store, cost is one of the first things to consider. So what do you think about which platform can perform better in this situation—WooCommerce or Shopify?

The answer is: it depends!

Most individuals turn to WooCommerce because it is cheaper and free to use, while Shopify costs from $25 to $399 per month and takes between 0.5 and 2% commission on your sales. Let’s take a deeper look:

WooCommerce

As we already discussed, WooCommercce is a free WordPress plugin. That makes it more affordable than Shopify. However, you need to pay its other costs, such as plugins, themes, and hosting plans. 

For example, Cloudways, a well-known cloud hosting platform, offers hosting plans starting at $11 per month. The paid theme at WooCommerce can range from $30 to $300, while plugins and add-ons average around $50. 

These additional paid features from WooCommerce can increase your expenses when starting or running your online store with this platform.

Shopify

It provides users with cost-effective functionality. It is an easy method of website development that includes everything you need in its pricing plan to build an online platform. Additionally, you don’t need to invest your money in third-party hosting, themes, or plugins. 

However, pricing plans range from $9 to $299 per month. Apart from that, it also takes a transaction fee whenever any payment is made through third-party payment gateways.

Overall, Woocommerce is an excellent option because it comes with its free version and doesn’t take any transaction fees. However, it is important to calculate all the costs that you will have to invest in its other facilities, including hosting, themes, and plugins. Shopify includes everything in its one pricing, but as you scale your business, your expenses will be increasing equally.
