Why Shopify Is The Best Ecommerce Platform Today

05 May, 2017

At present, lots of businesses are playing an important role in the economy, and some of them are growing fast while some are falling in terms of profit. Establish and running a business in this highly competitive market is very difficult and challenging today. You have to do the hard work and time to make a name among the target market.

Suppose you want to open a retail store, and for this, you need a huge amount of money, which everyone can’t afford so what to do now. How you can open a store, where you can sell your services and products and make some profits. The answer to all these problems is “E-commerce”. Several eCommerce solutions are available for your business like Opencart, Magento, etc., but Shopify is the best eCommerce platform for you. Shopify is one of the world’s leading e-commerce platform and used by more than 100,000 active retailers across the world.

What is Shopify?

Shopify is an absolute e-commerce solution and lets you set up an online store to sell your items or products. You can completely customize the look of your website, organize all of your products, accept payments with credit cards as well as communicate with your customers. Shopify has full PCI Level 1 Compliant check out frameworks, so keep both you and your customers secure.

Reasons On Why  Shopify  The Best  Ecommerce Platform Listed Below:

Easy To Use, Affordable, And Cost-Effective

It is easy to use and cost-effective than any other eCommerce platform. You will be able to easily maintain your store without any problem. The cost of using the Shopify platform starts at  $29/month and is well worth the cost. What you say!

No Need To Learn How To Code

With Shopify platform, you don’t need to learn how to code. There are many reliable Shopify Development companies and you need to hire one of them. Your hired company will design your store in a way that lets you to easily add new products to your store, update content on pages and maintain your blog without you having to learn any coding.

Shopify Can Be Used In Person

In case you sell products in person or you attend trade shows, then you can also use Shopify in person by using Shopify POSl. In addition to this, you can add the retail package to your online store and can maintain all of your inventory in one place too.  Shopify helps you sell not only online and offline but also on-the-go using powerful evolving technology.

Unmatchable Support

The support provided by the Shopify platform cannot be beaten. Additionally, if you encounter any problem with Shopify or you have any questions regarding online store operations, for example, Can I use a custom domain with Shopify or How do I add social media sites like Facebook, Twitter or social buttons to my website? then the support team is really great and eager to help you work through any issues you encounter with.

Built-In SEO Feature

Shopify has superior built-in SEO and this will make it easy for your store to list high on the search results as well as to get more and more sales. If you are selling products on other leading sites like Amazon, eBay, Flip-kart, etc., then you can use Shopify to integrate all of your listings just using any of the free or paid apps available in the app store. Remember, maintain all the products in one place as it will save loads of time.

Apart from this, Shopify includes an amazing blogging system not only to create blog posts but also share them with the world. Besides, this platform has built-in coupon codes as well as  A/B testing. It hosts your website on fast servers with cutting edge infrastructure. Also, Shopify websites let for unlimited stock keeping units (SKUs).   Shopify regularly adds free new features to help you sell products and services as well as to reach your market effectively.
